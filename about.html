About Us

The Gurgaon Industrial Association (GIA)

Empowering industries to innovate, expand, and excel in Gurugram's economic landscape.

Who We Are

The Gurgaon Industrial Association (GIA) is more than just an organization; it’s a collective voice and a powerful engine driving the industrial ecosystem in Gurugram. Established in 1968, GIA has been at the forefront of advocating for industrial growth, fostering collaboration, and ensuring sustainability in one of India’s fastest-growing industrial hubs.

At GIA, we believe in empowering industries to innovate, expand, and excel. From multinational corporations to small and medium enterprises, we represent a diverse network of businesses that form the backbone of Gurugram's economic landscape.
